Privacy Policy for CheerCart

Last updated: April 2026

CheerCart (the "Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

  • Search Queries: When you type a product search, we send that query to our backend to fetch results. We do not store search queries permanently unless you enable history (see below).
  • User Preferences: We store your theme preference (light/dark/system) and region selection locally in your browser using Chrome's storage.sync API. This data stays on your device and is not shared.
  • Optional History: If you choose to save your search history, we store your queries and the AI's responses in a secure cloud database (Supabase). You can view and delete this history at any time using the extension's "Clear History" button.
  • No Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, IP addresses, or any personally identifiable information (PII).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide product search results and recommendations.
  • To improve the relevance of our AI responses (anonymously, without identifying you).
  • To remember your preferences (e.g., dark mode).

3. Third-Party Services

  • eBay API: When you search, we send your search term to eBay to fetch product listings. eBay may collect data according to their own privacy policy.
  • Affiliate Networks: When you click a product link, you are redirected to the respective merchant (e.g., eBay). Those merchants may set cookies and collect data according to their own policies.
  • Supabase: If you enable history, we store your search queries and AI responses on Supabase's secure servers. Supabase is GDPR-compliant.

4. Your Choices

  • You can disable history at any time.
  • You can clear all stored history using the "Clear History" button in the extension popup.
  • You can uninstall the extension at any time; this will remove all locally stored data.

5. Children's Privacy

CheerCart is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. The latest version will always be posted on this page.
